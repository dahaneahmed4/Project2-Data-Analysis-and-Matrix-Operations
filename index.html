Part 3: Conceptual Questions

Interpretation & Application

1. What does the mean tell us?

The mean acts as the "center of gravity." For Product C, the mean of 11.25 indicates that on average, we expect to sell about 11 units per region. This is crucial for inventory forecasting.

2. Coefficient of Variation (CV)

CV = Std Dev / Mean. Product B has the lowest CV (0.22), meaning it is the most stable and reliable product regarding sales consistency.

3. Real-World Matrix Applications

Recommendation Systems: Netflix multiplies a User Matrix by a Movie Matrix to predict ratings.
Computer Graphics: Images are matrices. Matrix multiplication is used to rotate, scale, or apply filters to images.
